Kirwan's best kept secret

We are a small family-run local bakery in Kirwan, Queensland that started back in 2012. With the growing demand for something new and creative, we aspired to reinvent traditional Australian baked goods to cater to the changing taste of our generation.

You will never go wrong with our selection of donuts, tarts and cakes. Our artisan-baker trained in Paris and delivers one of the finest treats that you will find in town.

Located at Prize Lane, right next to the bustling Willows Shopping Center, we offer the perfect spot for your weekend catch ups or family day outs.

Our customers

With a nationwide following of customers such as James Cook University, the Cowboys and Telstra, our products have become a local sensation.
